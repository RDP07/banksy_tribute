"If graffiti changed anything it would be illegal -Banksy"

Banksy is the most controversial and notorious graffiti artist of all time. He has been in the shadows throughout the world for over 20 years, as his true idenity is still unknown. It is believe that he was born in Bristol, United Kingdom around 1977. Banksy truly pushes the envelope with his street art and makes viewers really question things like: life, government, honest, morality, and humanity. Banksy has become so infamous, when a piece of his is found inhabitants of the area flock to catch a glimpse. Some even try to remove and take pieces, as most of his pieces sold in the art market usally sell for around $500,000.
